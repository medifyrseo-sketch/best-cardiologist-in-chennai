## Symptoms That Should Never Be Ignored

You should consult a cardiologist immediately if you experience:

* Chest pain or discomfort
* Breathlessness during routine activities
* Irregular heartbeat
* Frequent dizziness
* Fainting episodes
* Swelling in legs or feet
* Persistent fatigue
* Pain spreading to the shoulder, jaw, or left arm

Prompt medical attention can prevent serious cardiovascular emergencies.
